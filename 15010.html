Rolex Oyster Perpetual Date – Ref. 15010

Description

The Rolex Oyster Perpetual Date 15010 is a classic 34mm stainless steel model, featuring a smooth engine-turned bezel, automatic movement, and elegant dial variations. Produced mainly during the 1980s, it remains one of the most iconic vintage Rolex Date models.

Specifications

  • Reference: 15010
  • Case size: 34mm
  • Material: Stainless Steel
  • Bezel: Engine-Turned Smooth Bezel
  • Movement: Automatic (Caliber 3035)
  • Crystal: Sapphire or Acrylic (depending on year)
  • Bracelet: Oyster or Jubilee
  • Year Range: 1980s
