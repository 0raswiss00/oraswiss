Returns Policy

General Return Conditions

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase from Ora Swiss. If you are not happy with your item, you may be eligible to return it under the conditions described below.

Return Period

You may request a return within 30 days from the date you receive your package, as confirmed by the carrier’s delivery information.

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, the item must:

  • Be in its original, unused condition
  • Include all original packaging, tags, and accessories
  • Include any certificates or documents provided with the watch (for example from The Watch Register or Enquirus, where applicable)
  • Not be damaged due to misuse, impact, or unauthorized repairs

Returns for Watches Above 1,500 €

For watches with a value above 1,500 €, any certificate from The Watch Register and any ownership transfer documentation from Enquirus must be returned together with the watch. Failure to return such documentation may affect your eligibility for a full refund.

Return Process

To start a return, please contact us with your order number and details about the item you wish to return. We will provide you with instructions on how and where to send your package. Returns sent without prior authorization may not be accepted.

Return Shipping Costs

Unless the return is due to our error (wrong item, defective item), the customer is generally responsible for the cost of return shipping.

Inspection of Returned Items

Once we receive your returned item, we will inspect it to ensure it meets our return conditions. If approved, the refund or exchange will be processed according to our Refund Policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about returns, please contact us through the contact details listed on our Contact page.
